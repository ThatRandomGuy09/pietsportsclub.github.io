About Us

WELCOME to the PIET SPORTS CLUB. This club includes all sports activities organized at COLLEGE level, JAIPUR level as well as STATE level. This club organizes all the inter college sports in various games like cricket, football, volleyball, basketball, table tennis, carrom, chess and bodybuilding. Sports and physical activity positively impacts academic performance of the student. Sports plays a vital role in all our life.....we all should play sports at least once in our life. It teaches us many real life lessons and skills which benefits us in applying them in our life. Sports teach TEAMWORK, PLANNING, LEADERSHIP, DEDICATED, HARDWORK, DISCIPLINE, TIME MANAGEMENT and URGE TO ACHIEVE A GOAL. SPORTS teach us how to win honorably, lose gracefully, respect authority, work with others, manage our time and stay out of trouble. So this club gives an opportunity to the students to enhance their sporting skills and showcase their talents on th field, help students not only in learning various techniques but also to develop sportman spirit, supports all those sportspersons and athletes in all possible ways to achieve their goals in college, city, state and national level also.
